The Better Experience Ecosystem

Better Experience wants to be an autonomous distributed organization creating and distributing the tools and infrastructure needed to build a better Tours and Activities ecosystem. Our collective goal is to involve all players in the market with in the attempt of solving current #1 problem, Increase Activities Online

Activity Suppliers

Defien their own website with direct booking, local activities suggestions (based on tourists preferences) and distribution to OTA and others sales channels at 0 cost.

Reservation Systems

Integrate existing systems with advanced tech services (e.g. local marketplace, artificial intelligence, voice recognition) reducing the technology gap with dominant OTA.

Online Travel Agencies

Integrate their product collection with an Open Data collection that is distributed only through all "Local OTA" - the independent activities website - increasing their sales point.

Distributors

Access to an Open API they can use to offer a well organised data set of Activities they can sell directly to their user base with direct booking capabilities.

Tourists

Enable the access to a global data set of Activities where trust and transparency are built into the technology and the way decisions are taken

Developers

Access to a clear, complete and documented open API that can be used to experiment new ideas and bring innovation in the Tours and activities.
